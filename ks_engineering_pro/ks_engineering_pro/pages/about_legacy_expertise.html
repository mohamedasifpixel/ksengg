Our Journey Through Time

Key milestones that shaped KS Engineering Pro into Chennai's trusted precision manufacturing partner

1995

Company Foundation

KS Engineering Pro was founded with a single lathe machine and an unwavering commitment to precision. Started operations in a 500 sq ft workshop in Chennai.

2000

First Major Partnership

Secured our first major contract with Brakes India Limited, marking the beginning of long-term OEM partnerships and establishing our reputation for quality.

2010

Major Expansion

Expanded to a 5000 sq ft facility with advanced CNC machines and wire cutting technology. Increased capacity to handle complex automotive components.

2020

Digital Transformation
